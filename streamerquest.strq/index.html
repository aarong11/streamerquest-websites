What is StreamerQuest?

StreamerQuest is an immersive application designed for aspiring and experienced streamers alike. It allows you to practice streaming in a simulated environment with AI-powered viewers. These AI viewers come with customizable personalities that influence how they interact with your streams, providing you with realistic feedback and diverse challenges. Whether you're testing out new content or improving your streaming style, StreamerQuest helps you hone your skills in a dynamic, risk-free environment.

Features

AI Viewers with Real Personalities

Each AI viewer in StreamerQuest has a unique, editable personality that determines how they behave during your streams. From supportive fans to tough critics, you'll encounter a wide range of interactions that mirror real-life streaming scenarios. Customize their personalities to create your ideal audience or challenge yourself with unpredictable viewer behavior.

Content Simulation Based on Viewer Personalities

The AI viewers not only react to your streams but also influence the type of content that emerges during your sessions. Their personalities shape the discussions, requests, and even the memes that appear in chat, offering you a dynamic and engaging streaming experience.
